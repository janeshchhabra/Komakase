Komakase Coffee

Komakase is an intimate coffee omakase experience in San Francisco, showcasing the finest specialty coffees from around the world. Each curated course highlights unique brewing methods and flavor profiles, paired with small bites and palate cleansers to enhance the journey. Perfect for coffee lovers and the curious alike!

About Us

Komakase is more than just a coffee shop; it's a journey through the world of coffee. Led by a dynamic duo, Josh and Kieran, we're passionate about sharing our love for coffee and showcasing the incredible range of flavors it offers.  

Josh, a seasoned home coffee enthusiast, brings years of experience and a deep appreciation for the art of coffee brewing.

Kieran, a seasoned coffee professional, offers a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the industry. Together, they've combined their skills to create a unique coffee omakase experience.

Indulge in our curated coffee flights, where we'll guide you through a selection of exceptional beans, expertly brewed to highlight their individual characteristics. Each cup is a story waiting to be discovered.

Our Omakase

Komakase offers two selections, the Omakase, or Roaster's Spotlight. Standard Omakase ($65) consists of five beverages: two pour overs, two mocktails — like an espresso old fashioned or an espresso tonic — and a spritz. Meanwhile the roaster spotlight ($70) includes an additional pour over, a shot of espresso and a piccolo (small shot with steamed milk). Every service, the roaster, food pairings and mocktails might change but the layout of the menu will remain.
